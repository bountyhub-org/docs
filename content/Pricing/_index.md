# Free for now

Currently, the application is free. The parts of it will still remain free after
the pricing model is established. You will always be able to create some scans
you won't be billed on.

# For the future

As the platform will not display targeted advertisements, the pricing model will
likely be based on the amount of data stored.

Execution time will not factor into the pricing as it occurs on the user's machine.
However, the cost of storing data on the servers will increase as more data is stored.

The pricing model will be determined after the v1 release of the app, but I can assure you
that it will not be expensive for fellow hackers worldwide.
While I do need to charge for running and developing this platform, I will ensure that the cost is reasonable.
